# Prediction of client subscription to a bank term deposit (https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/222/bank+marketing)

## Business Objective 

We have been given data from direct marketing campaigns (phone calls) of a Portuguese banking institution. The classification goal is to predict if the client will subscribe a term deposit through these telemarketing calls.

## Classification result 
After cleaning and data tranformation, various classification models were compared, namely Logistic Regression, SVM,Decision Trees and KNN.
It was determined that SVM gave the most balanced results.
